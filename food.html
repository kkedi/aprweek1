CLASSES OF FOOD

What are the classes of food and their sources? There are seven major classes of food based on their nutrient properties:

Carbohydrates

A carbohydrate is a biomolecule consisting of carbon, hydrogen and oxygen atoms, usually with a hydrogen–oxygen atom ratio of 2:1 and thus with the empirical formula C
  • Grains

Protein

Proteins are large biomolecules and macromolecules that comprise one or more long chains of amino acid residues.

  • Egg

Fats

Fats in our body are made up of three molecules joined together. This three-molecule structure is called a “triglyceride”.

  • Vegetable

Fiber

Fiber is a type of indigestible carbohydrate. Learn more about how fiber is useful for regulating blood sugar and keeping hunger in check

  1. Broccoli

Mineral

vital for improving bodily functions, are present in most foods, fruits, and vegetables

Vitamins

a pill containing a specified amount of a particular vitamin or vitamins, taken as a dietary supplement
